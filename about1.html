Features of DATUM

This website is designed specifically for educational institutes keeping in mind their needs and demands.We have worked even on minute details to make the user experience more dynamic and easy. The vast amount of information generated by every student can systematically and efficiently be updated on the DATUM, thereby facilitating the administration to manage as well as organize all kinds of information, ready to be used as and when needed. A student's progress can also be tracked with the help of various analytic tools and dashboards available.
